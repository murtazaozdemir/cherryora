Romanticize Your Life
One Cherry Moment at a Time 🍒

Amid stress and busy schedules, it can be difficult to embrace life fully. But romanticizing your life helps you appreciate the little things and find joy in everyday moments. Let's dive in!

What Does Romanticizing Your Life Mean?

Oxford Definition

"To deal with or describe in an idealistic or unrealistic fashion; make (something) seem better or more appealing than it really is."

In Practice

Making every day and mundane things seem more special — whether it's your skincare routine, getting dressed for class, studying in the library, or grabbing coffee.

A Shift in Perspective

Seeing yourself as the main character of your life, while practicing mindfulness and finding appreciation in everyday moments.

How To Romanticize Your Life 🍒

Three simple practices to transform your daily routine

01

☀️ Morning Routine

Start each day with intention! Make your bed for a sense of accomplishment, follow with skincare, and style your hair. Remember: "If you look good, you feel good."

02

💪 Working Out

Transform exercise from a chore into an experience. Wear outfits that make you feel confident, try different classes, and discover what brings you joy in movement.

03

📚 Homework & Studying

Create a cozy environment! Use cute stationery, light a candle, find a cute cafe, or grab an iced coffee. Make your study space feel aesthetic and inviting.

"Romanticizing your life is a form of mindfulness — a shift in perspective that allows you to appreciate everyday life and mundane things."

- Samantha Vincenty, SELF Magazine
